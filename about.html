About the studio

A group of young architects, committed to what we build.

Founded in 2023 to provide planning, architectural and project management services to developers and the public — and to prove that restraint, done well, endures.

Our story

Arkitek Tan & Tan Sdn Bhd was founded in 2023 to serve developers and the public across planning, architecture and project management. In a short time it has overseen developments of varying scale — from residential bungalows to commercial schemes and townships.

Our portfolio covers a wide spectrum of building types: residential, resort & hospitality, commercial and industrial. We operate as a small consultancy of fifteen full-time personnel, a team of young professional architects, each committed to improving the quality of architecture around us.

We specialise in practicality with a touch of creativity in every aspect of design and planning. Our philosophy and methodology are honed to optimise time, space and resource — and in doing so, to achieve complete satisfaction for our clients and for ourselves.

Philosophy

Clarity before spectacle.

We design with quiet confidence, balancing precision with warmth, technology with humanity, and timeless simplicity with thoughtful innovation.

We embrace restraint — not as an aesthetic trend, but as a discipline that allows light, nature, craftsmanship and human experience to take precedence.

Mission

The most conducive environment its resources allow.

Our mission is to always create the most conducive environmental ambience within the context of a project's given resources — helping both our partners and end users feel wholly satisfied in their newfound environment.

We help clients realise the best potential of their property in aesthetic appeal, ambience, spatial usage and monetary return. That balance — between beauty and value — is the work.
